# Immortality

A practical immortality mod for **Crusader Kings III**.

This mod lets you grant, change, remove, and manage immortality for yourself and other characters through decisions and character interactions. It is meant to be a usable immortality toolbox rather than a balance-focused overhaul.

Steam Workshop: https://steamcommunity.com/sharedfiles/filedetails/?id=3716732113

## Features

- Add immortality to your character
- Grant immortality to other characters
- Choose between standard, genetic, and sterile immortality
- Change an existing immortal character’s immortality type
- Remove immortality
- Add disease immunity to immortal characters
- Set or freeze an immortal character’s apparent age
- Shift age younger or older
- Restore body and mind by removing negative health effects such as wounds and diseases
- Enable or disable yearly automatic body and mind restoration
- Reset selected once-in-a-lifetime lockouts for immortal characters
- Hide or show the immortality decisions and interactions when you do not need them

## Immortality Types

### Standard Immortality

The character stops aging and can still have children, but their children will be mortal.

### Genetic Immortality

The character stops aging and can pass immortality to their children.

### Sterile Immortality

The character stops aging but can no longer have children.

## Body and Mind Restoration

Immortal characters can restore their body and mind, removing many negative health effects such as wounds and diseases.

This can be done manually, or enabled as a yearly automatic effect.

## Age Control

Immortal characters can have their apparent age set or shifted.

This lets you freeze a character at a chosen age, make them younger, make them older, or fine-tune their age after becoming immortal.

## Once-in-a-Lifetime Lockouts

Because immortal characters can live far beyond a normal lifespan, this mod includes tools to reset selected once-in-a-lifetime lockouts, including:

- Kingdom invasions
- Kingdom subjugation wars
- Kingdom holy wars
- Faith creation
- Culture conversion
- Lifestyle perk resets
- Vassal contract modification blocks

## Compatibility

Built and tested for **Crusader Kings III 1.19.\***.

This mod mainly adds new traits, decisions, interactions, events, scripted effects, and icons. It should be compatible with many mods, but any mod that heavily changes immortality traits, disease immunity, age handling, health effects, or character interaction behavior may need testing.
